Design and creativity


In the course I have learned how to express myself visualy and what things to take into concideration when drawing and structuring a poster. Furthermore I have learned new creative techniques which were completely new for me.

This was one of our first assingments for Design and creativity class. The challange was to draw the storry of my life. During this challange we learned how to structurise the content from most relevant to least relevant, furthermore, we have learned the most basic design rule 3-30-300.

This assingment was to make a poster of our trip to Austria in which we visited ars electronica festival of arts. This assingment helped me to further develop my skills of sketching and structurising information.

After quick brainstorm I decided to go for something more interesting than just a box with my skills and advantages writen on it. The plan was to create something more dificult with methaphorycal meaning behind it that at the same time would showcase my creativity and craftsmenship.

I came up with the idea of creating a Trojan horse which would represent me. The dangerous army inside capable of destroying cities perfectly portrays my skills and my ability to deal with any task given to me.
